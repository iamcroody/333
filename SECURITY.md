## Reporting a Vulnerability

As an advocate for the open-source community and ethical hacking, I take security very seriously. If you have discovered a vulnerability in this platform (including the simulated encrypted filesystem, WASM tensor visualizers, or the hidden blockchain engine), please follow the responsible disclosure process.

1. **Do NOT** open a public issue.
2. Email your findings to `jajimenez4@eafit.edu.co` with the subject `[SECURITY VULNERABILITY] - [Type]`.
3. Provide a detailed Proof of Concept (PoC) and steps to reproduce.
4. I will acknowledge receipt within 24 hours and aim to provide a patch within 72 hours.

## Bug Bounty
